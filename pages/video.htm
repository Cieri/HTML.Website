Thatz'a Battle!

In early 2017, Thatz'a Pizza was welcomed onto CBS 21's set to participate in their exclusive pizza competition. Owner Kevin Shutt stood alongside some well-known TV faces like Tom Russel and shared our life’s passion: pizza!

CBS 21's Contest

While many contestants brought the tried and true Neapolitan style pizza to battle, we entered with our signature Tomato Pie and made a lasting impression on the judges. We had the honor of not only going up against long-standing establishments, like Al’s Pizza and Jojo’s Pizza, but besting them as well.

Some time after the pizza gurus gathered at CBS 21’s station, Thatz’a Pizza was announced as the Viewer’s Choice, and we can only attribute this to the impressions we’d made and friendships we’d cultivated with our customers and our community.

Thatz'a Win!

At the time of the competition, Thatz’a Pizza was less than three years old, and we were stunned to win such an award at such an early stage, but we are proud and incredibly thankful, nonetheless.
